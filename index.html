YAWN "Sleep is the best meditation"

Yawn has been designed with the purpose to help those people that have trouble relaxing and falling asleep caused by an accumulation or miss handling of stress and anxiety. Most people tend to rely on watching television, playing video games or even chatting and spending hours scrolling through social media sites. What this does is take your mind away from the real problem that needs solving, learning to control your emotions and working on your sleep pattern and in fact makes the situation worse as these activities require your attention for long periods of time but do not eliminate the emotional blockage causing these symptoms and difficulty to fall asleep due to stress and anxiety.

Features

  • A wide range of nature sounds.
  • Personalized compositions
  • Recommendation of sounds according to registered mood or feeling.
  • Have control over the sleep routine by having registered sleep patterns.

HOMEFY - For Tenants

Homefy allows you to manage your home quickly, clearly and accurately saving you time and misunderstandings with your landlord. Use the app from minute one to sign your contract digitally from anywhere and inform and carry out the follow-up of any incident that may arise in the home and do not worry, as you will always have access to all agreements and incidents with your landlord.

  • Sign your contract digitally and securely.
  • Warn of incidents from the app, include photos and consult any agreement made with the landlord whenever you want.
  • Control your expenses and Payments.

HOMEFY - For Landlords

Homefy allows you to manage all your properties together, giving you the control of all of them from the App making your life easier. At a glance you can see the pending payments of your tenants, the status of incidents and communicate directly with them and privately.

  • Sign digitally the contract saving you unnecessary trips and arranging of them in an organized way.
  • Inform the tenant of the state of their possible incidents and maintain a history of all of them.
  • Set automated alerts to notify your tenants in case of late payments.

LA CASA DEL LIBRO - Taking the Web to the App.

The website we chose was “La Casa del Libro”, a well known bookstore in Spain with over 47 physical stores in different cities around Spain.
The first thing we found when we began our research was that “La Casa del Libro” already has an existing App. The problem we found with the app was that it was an exact replica from the website, this had not been adapted to a mobile version therefore we found the same problem as the website: Way too much information, the search process was very long and confusing to the users and the information architecture was unorganized.

Design Solutions

  • Redesign the app and adapt it to mobile version
  • Restructure and organize the information in the app for a better flow when it comes to searching by eliminating the marketing banners and reducing the content of the app.
  • Design a purchase process more intuitive for the user to be able to access the product information in an easier manner by having all the information necessary by having it all in one place and nicely organized and with a clear visual of what is available and be able to filter by choice.
